Chole Bhature

A Classic North Indian Delight

Ingredients

For Chole (Chickpea Curry):

  • 1 cup dried chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 medium tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

For Bhature (Fried Bread):

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp semolina (sooji)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Preparation Steps

Preparing Chole:

  1. Pressure cook the soaked chickpeas with salt until soft (about 4-5 whistles).
  2. In a pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  6. Add turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add the cooked chickpeas along with some of the cooking water. Simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  8. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.

Preparing Bhature:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine flour, semolina, baking soda, and salt.
  2. Add yogurt and mix well. Add water gradually to form a soft dough.
  3. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for 2 hours.
  4. Divide the dough into equal portions and roll them into balls.
  5. Roll each ball into a round disc using a rolling pin.
  6. Heat oil in a deep frying pan. Fry each disc until it puffs up and turns golden brown.
  7. Remove and drain on paper towels.
