Chole Bhature Recipe

Ingredients for Chole (Chickpeas Curry)

  • 1 cup chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp chole masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Instructions for Chole

  1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with water and a pinch of salt until soft.
  2. Heat oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  3. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, cook for a minute.
  5. Stir in tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  6. Add turmeric, red chili powder, chole masala, garam masala, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add cooked chickpeas along with some water. Simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  8. Garnish with coriander leaves and set aside.

Ingredients for Bhature

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp semolina (sooji)
  • 1/4 cup curd (yogurt)
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions for Bhature

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, semolina, baking powder, baking soda, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add curd and mix to form a soft dough using water as needed.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rest for 2–3 hours.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls and roll them into circles.
  5. Heat oil in a deep pan and fry the bhature until golden and puffed.
  6. Drain on paper towels.

Serve hot Chole with fluffy Bhature and enjoy!
